Meet Waterlily!

Founded on a passion for innovation and a commitment to sustainability, Waterlily Bicycles has been at the forefront of cycling excellence since 2019. Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize the biking experience, combining state-of-the-art technology with eco-friendly practices.

At Waterlily Bicycles, we believe in the power of two wheels to change the world. Our bicycles are designed with precision engineering and a focus on rider experience. From the seamless integration of advanced gear systems to the aerodynamic frames that slice through the air, every aspect of a Waterlily bike is crafted for optimal performance.

  • Advanced carbon fiber frames for lightweight durability.
  • Innovative gear systems for smooth and efficient riding.
  • Eco-friendly production processes that reduce environmental impact.

  • Enhanced riding comfort and control.
  • Superior performance in various terrains and conditions.
  • A sustainable choice for eco-conscious cyclists.

  • Frame: Carbon Fiber T700/T800
  • Gears: Shimano Ultegra 11-speed
  • Brakes: Hydraulic Disc Brakes
  • Wheels: 700c Alloy Rims

Join us in our mission to create a greener, healthier world—one ride at a time.
